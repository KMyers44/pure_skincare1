Information about Aesthetics

Aesthetics is a branch of philosophy dealing with taste and the study of beauty, physically or in nature and art. An Aesthetician has a passion for the beauty of the skin and is well trained in the cosmetic treatment of the skin.

Pure skincare Aesthetics is based on the belief that beauty starts from within. A healthy mind and body reflects on your skin first, being it is our largest organ.
